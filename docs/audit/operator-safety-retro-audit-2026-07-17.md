## P0 — silent books corruption (fix before the next pilot posts real data) (1)

### A194. Realized-FX block in receipts/payments emits unbalanced POSTED journal entries — ✅ FIXED 2026-07-17
**Fix:** the bank/cash line now keeps the actual cash (`functional_amount`); the AR/AP counterparty line is adjusted by the (correctly-computed, foreign-based) realized FX; the realized-FX account resolves via core mapping → FINANCIAL_INCOME/EXPENSE role fallback → else refuse; and an unconditional pre-emit guard refuses any entry where `abs(debit − credit) > 0.05`. Test: `backend/tests/test_a194_fx_receipt_payment_balance.py` (9 cases incl. gain/loss both sides, partial-allocation advance, role fallback, and a direct guard test).
- **Rule:** R2 stop-loudly-not-guess | **Cluster:** accounting-core | **Site:** `backend/accounting/commands.py:3860`
- **Defect:** record_customer_receipt and record_vendor_payment build JOURNAL_ENTRY_POSTED events directly, bypassing post_journal_entry's validation, and the realized-FX adjustment is broken two ways: (a) unit mixing - lines[0]['debit'] = str(receipt_amount + realized_fx_total) (commands.py:3860, :3874; payment mirror :4332, :4346) overwrites the bank line's FUNCTIONAL-currency amount with the FOREIGN receipt amount plus a functional FX delta; (b) partial mapping - the guard at :3855/:4328 passes when EITHER of REALIZED_FX_GAIN/REALIZED_FX_LOSS is mapped, adjusts the bank line, then the inner 'if realized_gain_account/realized_loss_account' drops the offsetting line. Totals are recomputed (:3898-3899, :4371-4372) but never compared debit==credit before emit, and the projection applies posted events with no balance check (backend/projections/accounting.py:436-481).
- **Failure scenario:** Merchant on an EGP-functional company posts a USD invoice (booked @47), then records a USD 100 receipt @48 allocated to it via the /accounting/receipts page -> the bank debit is computed from the foreign amount (~200) while the AR credit stays functional (~4800), and the unbalanced JOURNAL_ENTRY_POSTED event is emitted and projected -> GL account balances and the trial balance are silently corrupted by roughly the receipt's whole functional value, with no ProjectionFailureLog/exception row. Same outcome in any currency when only one of the two realized-FX accounts is mapped: the bank line moves by the FX delta and the offset line is silently dropped.
